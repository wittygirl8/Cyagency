About Workshop

A workshop on "ETHICAL HACKING & CYBER SECURITY" presented by CYAGENCY .

Ethical Hacking and VAPT (vulnerability assessment and penetration testing) course is an exceptional kick-off for aspiring students to become a Cyber Security Expert in any successful IT firm all over the world.

For VAPT, you need to think like a Hacker and CYAGENCY will focus on both VAPT and Ethical Hacking during the course of the workshop as the two days workshop is particularly designed for the students who want to build a career in this field .We will teach students to expose all the security defects/breach present in the application by actually performing the attack and train how to provide solutions to defects.

Workshop Benefits

  • 1. Exposure to Advanced Hacking and Penetration testing Tools.

  • 2. A Complete Idea to build a path of becoming a Cyber Expert.

  • 3. How to think like a Hacker and secure yourself .

  • 4. Career guidance and scope of cyber security by experienced faculty.

  • 5. Each student will get Certificate of Participation from CYAGECNY.

WORKSHOP DURATION : 2 DAYS (14 HOURS)
